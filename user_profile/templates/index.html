Verified Profiles

To ensure that the information added by the student is not fake, a system of verification is used for every update a student makes to his/her profile.

Three Level of User Permissions:

A superuser, a teacher and the student. The student has the most basic access rights, the teacher verifies student information.

Dashboard for Student and Teachers

A dashboard facility with interactive forms for students and teachers to regularly update profiles.

Filter-based Results

For ease of use by teachers and recruiters, profiles can be filtered based on area of interest, research aptitude, projects, competitive coding skills etc.

Profile Updation and Analysis

Whenever a student updates his/her profile, it can be viewed by all as a student progress in the respective domain. Analysis is carried out to show how much progress is carried it in the domain in how much time.

Connecting teachers and students:

When students take up projects under a college professor, the system users can view the professor portfolio and project details on the portal.
